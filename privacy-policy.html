Privacy Policy

Station Commissioning — by Blugraph

Last updated: 29 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Station Commissioning app ("App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our Android application and the associated web application at blugraph.github.io/commissioning-app.

1. Who We Are

Station Commissioning is developed and operated by Blugraph.
Contact: blugraphtech@gmail.com

2. What the App Does

Station Commissioning is a field tool used by authorised engineers to record, document, and save commissioning data for water level monitoring stations. It reads station reference data from a private Google Sheet, guides the engineer through a structured inspection form, captures site photos, and saves the completed report as a Google Doc in a designated Google Drive folder.

3. Information We Collect

The App collects only the information necessary to produce commissioning reports:

  • Station data — station ID, name, and survey measurements read from a private Google Spreadsheet. This data is not collected from users; it is pre-existing reference data.
  • Inspection inputs — measurements, indicator selections, and water level readings entered by the engineer during commissioning.
  • Photos — site and equipment photos captured or selected from the device gallery by the engineer. Photos are processed entirely on-device (resized) and sent directly to the report document. They are not stored on our servers.
  • Device camera & storage access — the App requests permission to use the camera and access the photo gallery solely to capture photos for the commissioning report.

The App does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, device identifiers, usage analytics, or any other personal information from users.

4. How We Use Information

Information entered in the App is used solely to:

  • Generate a commissioning report document in Google Docs format.
  • Save that document to a designated Google Drive folder.

We do not use any information for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Third-Party Services

Completed reports are saved to Google Drive using the Google Docs and Drive APIs. By using the App, you acknowledge that report data (including photos) is transmitted to and stored in Google Drive under the account configured by your organisation. Google's own privacy policy applies to data stored in Google Drive: policies.google.com/privacy.

The App uses a Google Apps Script backend solely to copy the report template and embed photos into the document. No data is retained by this backend beyond the duration of the request.

All other data processing (resizing photos, computing measurements, rendering the form) happens entirely on your device.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties. Report documents are saved to Google Drive; access to those documents is controlled by the folder sharing settings configured by your organisation.

7. Data Retention

The App stores a session token and the Apps Script endpoint URL in your device's local storage for convenience. No report data is retained in local storage after the session. Completed reports are stored in Google Drive indefinitely until deleted by the folder administrator.

8. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for use by professional engineers and authorised personnel only. It is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Security

All communication between the App and Google services uses HTTPS. Photos and report data are transmitted directly to Google's infrastructure and are not stored on any intermediate server operated by us.
